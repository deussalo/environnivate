Who We Are

Environnivate is a Western Australian based Environmental Consultancy specialising in environmental compliance management. Andrea Wills has over 20 years of industry experience across all facets of industry projects activities (planning, construction, operations, decommissiong, rehabilitation).

Environnivate was started to provide an economical all-encompassing environmental service company to industry. The name reflects the environmentally innovative service that is provided to the customer.

Andrea Wills

Andrea is a degree qualified Environmental Engineer and post graduate qualified in Natural Resources Law and Occupational Health and Safety. Andrea is an Exemplar Global certified Lead Environmental and Quality Auditor. Andrea has undertaken over 100 audits including ISO14001, Ministerial Statement Compliance Assessment Reporting, EPBC Annual Compliance Assessment Reports and other Environmental Compliance Audits. 

Andrea has 20 years onshore Upstream Oil and Gas experience in environmental management including approvals and project implementation, compliance monitoring, reporting and rehabilitation. 

Andrea worked continuously on the ARC Energy Limited / AWE Limited North Perth Basin and Canning Basin assets from 2004 through 2019 on a full array of upstream petroleum projects (surveys, drilling, FEED, construction, operations, decommissioning and rehabilitation) in all phases of environmental status (planning, assessment, approvals, implementation, operation, compliance, reporting (including NGERS and NPI collation, calculation and reporting), remediation). Andrea undertook all environmental activities required to support and enhance continual environmental improvement in the organisation. Andrea visited the field approximately monthly. Andrea permitted (landowner and environmental) a 600 km2 gravity survey. 

While consulting for AGR / Oceaneering / Upstream PS from 2010 to 2019, Andrea also worked on additional facilities and projects associated with the Cliff Head offshore and onshore facilities and Red Gully activities. Andrea was responsible for developing Environment Plans, liaising with regulators, compliance auditing and NGERS and NPI collation, calculation and reporting. 

Andrea has consulted to Metgasco, RCMA and PBE Operations undertaking all aspects of their environmental approvals and compliance for the drilling and rehabilitation of the Cervantes 1 conventional oil exploration well.  

Andrea has consulted to Beach Energy on their 2022 to 2024 exploration drilling program and provides environmental support to Hancock Energy. 

Andrea has been involved in industrial projects including environmental planning of the IBE Hydrogen Project, due diligence at Perth Airport and compliance for Covalent Lithium Kwinana.
